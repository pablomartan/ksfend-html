Mathematics

Mathematics is a formal science. It studies numbers, relations, geometric shapes, and lots of other objects. It was invented, as a science, by the ancient greeks, that had a lot of free time and a passion for abstract thinking. What I find fascinating about maths, and also logic, is that it is all derivable from a (relatively) small set of axioms. Or at least so it seemed until K. Gödel proved it to be impossible for systems more powerful than first order logic, as maths are... It is fun nontheless.
