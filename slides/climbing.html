Climbing

Climbing is a risks sport. I like it because it places you, and only you, agains the wall. Even if this is so, it is also a very social sport. It is much funnier to climb with someone, try to figure out how to climb a wall or boulder togheter, and have fun in general.

You can practice it indoors. This is the most convenient way if you live in a city.

It can also be practiced outdoors, either bouldering, which is a recent modality...

...or lead, wich requires much more equipment, and is more dangerous as you climb a lot higher.
