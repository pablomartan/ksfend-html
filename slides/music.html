Music

Music is a performing art, whose main element is sound. We could say that music is 'arranging sounds along time'. Some of the music styles I like are Jazz, Funk and Indie Rock.

Jazz

Jazz is an improvisational kind of music. The band takes a song, usually 32 bars long, plays it, and then improvises based on the chords of the song.

Funk

Funk music is a genre with strong empahsis on syncopation and strong, sometimes repetitive rhythms, that invites to dance.

Indie Rock

Indie is short for 'independent'. It relates to the beginnings of the style, as the first bands were recorded and published by independent record labels. Nowadays it is as mainstream as any other genre.
